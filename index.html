Pattaya City Sign

It’s quite a hike to get up to the sign, however the reward is awesome views of Pattaya City. Most people don’t even realise you can go up there.

Pattaya Beach

Some people love it, some hate it. The water might not be the best but it still is a great place to relax to the sound of the ocean

Big Buddha Pattaya

Hilltop destination with a towering gold Buddha statue & awesome views of Pattaya Bay. The Buddha image is 18 meters tall. It is the largest Buddha image in Chonburi Province located on Pratumnak Hill.

Soi 6 Pattaya

Infamous 300 meter strip filled with Beer Bars, Ladies and pumping music. (Never a dull moment!)

Jomtien Beach

As you can see it is much longer than Pattaya Beach and a lot more sand between the beach chairs and the sea.

Jomtien Pattaya Beach Sign

Not to be outdone by the big Pattaya sign on the hill Jomtien has its own.
