Central Festival Pattaya

CentralFestival Pattaya Beach is The largest beach front shopping mall in Asia. Plenty of high end stores as well as Restaurants and cinema..

Central Festival By Night

A high end shopping center inside but by night a vibrant market with plenty of bargains as well as some street food and usually a "Cocktail Car" to relax with a cold one and some music.

Beach Road Pattaya

Anyone who has visited Pattaya will be familiar with this view opposite Central Festival Pattaya

Royal Garden Plaza

Popular spot to pose for a photo thanks to the crashing aeroplane also a great spot to shop and play.

Pattaya Beer Garden

A great place to relax by the sea with a great view of the Pattaya Sign. They have some great food here from breakfast to dinner, also a great place at night

Wave Hotel Pattaya

There are so many hotels in pattaya but this one really stands out with it's bright colour and pink Cadillac parked at the front..
